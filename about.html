Meet the team

We are Ryan Hecht, Raghu Nimmagadda, Jonghyun Lee, and Isaac Benghiat, a group of first-year Brown students participating in our very first Hackathon.

Memeaction was born from the idea that a picture is worth a thousand words--and gif's are made up of multiple pictures, so what better way to convey the nuance of emotion than through this information-dense medium?

Take a picture conveying your current mood--happy, sad, angry, or maybe something in between. We'll serve you up four gifs that will perfectly match your mood, or your money back!*
